I think both DSS and Expert systems can help managers. Expert Systems research provides one source of technologies for building Decision Support Systems I call them Knowledge Driven DSS. Integration of Artificial Intelligence and Expert System technologies may occur in some other types of DSS. DSS is a broad category that includes Data-Driven DSS, Model-Driven, Communication-Driven, Document-Driven and Knowledge-Driven DSS. The latter categories are built using Expert Systems tools. Expert Systems are man-machine systems with specialized problem-solving expertise. The "expertise" consists of knowledge about a particular domain, understanding of problems within that domain, and "skill" at solving some of these problems.
